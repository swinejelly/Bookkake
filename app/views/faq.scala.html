FAQ

Account

Your account is automatically created for you when you visit us with your WEBAUTH credentials. There is no need to sign in or remember a password outside of your CSH credentials.

Visit the profile page on the navbar to see your transaction history and add additional information, such as your current room/address and your real name! You can also visit other member's pages to see their information and transactions.

Books

You can enter your own books on the main page, as well as loan or give them to other members. To loan/give a book, click on the book's give/loan link on the index page, enter the receiving member's CSH username, select a due date or enter a price if applicable, and submit. Any price information/due-dates are solely the concerns of the participating parties; Bookkake only stores this information for reference.

It says it can't find the other user! What's up with that?

Bookkake is not case sensitive regarding users, but a user is only created when they access Bookkake. Have him/her visit the website first and make sure you are using a CSH username.

I can't see where it says to return a book! Where do I return my book?

The person who gives/lends the book is responsible for marking when it is returned. They can do so on the index page next to where their book is shown.
